  • Basics

  • When is the event?

    The event will begin from 8th August 2020.

  • Who will be participating in this event?

    This event is for class 9,10,11,12, AS and A-Level students

  • Is 2000 E batch eligible to participate in this event?

    No, 2000 E batch students will not be participating in this event.

  • What device is to be used during the event?

    You can use any device as long as it has a camera and a microphone.

  • How can I contact the organizers?

    If you have any further query, you can mail us at physicsolympics2k20@gmail.com

  • How will the winners be awarded?

    Winners will be awarded authentic digital certificates of their respective category i.e.
    i) Gold Category
    ii) Silver Category
    iii) Bronze Category
    iv) Honorable mention

  • Tutoring

  • Will the tutoring class start on August 8 itself?

    No, the tutoring class will formally begin from August 9 and there will be an orientation program on August 8.

  • What is the tutoring platform?

    Tutoring will be conducted via Zoom.

  • How many days a week will there be tutoring?

    Tutoring will be 5 days a week (Sunday-Thursday) and Friday will be a leave day.

  • What is the time duration for each tutoring class?

    Tutoring classes will be for 2 hours.

  • What if I miss my tutoring class due to some unavoidable situations?

    You will need to contact the organizers within 24 hours and state a valid reason for your absence.

  • What if I cannot inform the organizers regarding my absence?

    If someone fails to contact the organizers within 24 hours of their absence s/he will be disqualified from the event.

  • Tests

  • When will the mock test be conducted?

    Mock tests will be conducted on Saturdays.

  • How many tests will be conducted altogether?

    Two mock tests and a final test will be conducted.

  • Can everyone take part in the final test?

    Top 25 participants will be selected from the mock test results and they will be taking the final test.
